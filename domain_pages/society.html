NSS BLOG

VISION

NSS IIT Delhi maintains a blog website with the primary aim to spread awareness on a plethora of social causes including education, health, environment and society. Through this active project, we strive to provide correct information to our readers to highlight pertinent social issues.

DESCRIPTION

Volunteers write regular content for the site, such as articles, satire pieces, comics, and poems. The articles can also be contributed to the site by anyone. The articles are required to be researched well and be free of political bias. Each article is reviewed by someone in the team, to make sure the content is free from errors. The main work is popularising the social media front and publicity of the blog, side by side making new content about our own projects to display on the site, with regular articles on site.

Gender Mainstreaming

VISION

Through this project, we try to analyse and sensitise people about questionable stereotypes and notions relating to gender and sex. We initiate discussions on topics like equality, stereotypes, and discrimination based on gender identity and sexuality.

DESCRIPTION

The project primarily focuses on teaching about and sensitising people towards all the genders and clearing their myths and queries through fun and engaging activities with the help of experts in the field. The project tries to provide a platform for everyone to open up and share their past experiences of any discrimination, stereotypes or inequalities that they might have faced due to their gender.

Old Age Home

VISION

The primary aim of the Old Age Home Project is to provide mental support and love to the elderly people residing in old age homes. Through this project, we want to make the students realise the importance of elderly people in our lives and how blissful it is to bring a smile to someone’s face just by spending some time with them.

DESCRIPTION

In the offline tenure things used to be different and more impactful. In online tenure, we started a new initiative - “Letters of Hope” in which volunteers sent handwritten letters to them to provide them some emotional support. We also started an initiative- “Give them back” to encourage people to do something for their grandparents. We also conducted some online interactive fun sessions with the people of old age home which was a great success. As we will be returning to the offline mode, we’ll have regular visits to the old age homes on some weekends, festivals and birthdays to share some moments of joy with the elderly people. Along with this, the activities started in online tenure will also be continued further.

Green Warrior + E-Waste

VISION

The project Green Warriors is aimed towards the betterment of the environment by raising awareness and by conducting on-ground activities to make the campus greener. We also focus on the less-known issues like the effects of E-waste and employ innovative methods to spread the message of a cleaner and greener planet.

DESCRIPTION

In this project, we sensitize people about environment conservation sustainability issues as well as present solutions to their day-to-day wastage problems and connect them to nature. Along with a group of enthusiastic volunteers, we organise plantation drives, E-Waste collection drives, webinars, competitions and DIY activities related to the environment domain to tackle environmental issues.
