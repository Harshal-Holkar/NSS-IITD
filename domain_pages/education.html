ANKURAM

VISION

We believe literacy has the potential to meet people’s most vital needs and to stimulate social, cultural, political and economic participation. Adult education is distinct from child education hence different methodologies are adopted to teach them. Ankuram aims to increase the quality of life of an individual and emunirkale him/her to realise his/her potential for self-realisation and to raise their standard of living by implementing adult literacy programs.

DESCRIPTION

This project primarily focuses on adult literacy, with a special emphasis on women's education and empowerment. Regular classes and tests are given to find out how much they understand and help them improve. In previous tenure, we taught English to women in order to improve their communication skills and help them perform better on their chores. The curriculum includes courses such as Speaking English, Computer and Technology, Gender Awareness, finance, composing mail, data entry, basic maths, etc. Currently we are in collaboration with VIDYA Ngo for this initiative.

Aarohan

VISION

Our Team is driven to bridge the educational gaps that exist between hundreds of poor students and their affluent peers. We aim to provide quality education and inculcate learning habits in students pursuing Engineering Entrances who neither can afford nor have access to these values in current society.

DESCRIPTION

We teach board as well as JEE level material consisting of Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics to students of Class 11th, 12th, and 12th Droppers. Regular classes were held between 6 and 9 pm. Volunteers also assist students in providing mentorship and guidance in their JEE preparation. Volunteers make exhaustive bilingual assignments for boards, JEE Mains, and JEE Advanced to help students understand and practise questions. Currently, we are in collaboration with NGOs like Saraswati Sewa Foundation (SSF), Edjustice, and VIDYA, who help us reach out to our beneficiaries. Now we have to switch to offline mode, so we have to look for an NGO in Delhi that needs volunteers for tutoring and mentoring students for their preparation of boards, JEE Mains and Advanced. We have to find the beneficiaries and then ideate and manage the whole process to ensure that offline classes are conducted regularly.

Vidya

VISION

Our prime focus at VIDYA is to teach students from classes 4th to 12th and inculcate the fundamental concepts of all subjects, especially mathematics and science along with conducting extra-curricular activities for their holistic development.

DESCRIPTION

VIDYA Teaching Project is a collaboration with VIDYA NGO where a team of dedicated volunteers takes regular classes, teaching students of classes 4-12 especially Science and Mathematics at Kendriya Vidyalaya School on the IIT Delhi Campus. Classes are held from 3-5 pm on weekdays for the students. We make assignments, host fun activities, and take regular assessment exams while holding regular teaching 6 days a week.

Jigyasa

VISION

The project Jigyasa aims to teach students, especially girls from underprivileged communities, software programming languages such as Python, JavaScript, HTML/CSS and support them in getting an aspirational career. This project combines the field of Education and Technology and tries to bring them to the students.

DESCRIPTION

Project Jigyasa is a collaboration with NavGurukul NGO, where a dedicated team of volunteers takes regular classes, doubt sessions and mentor students of age group 16-25 years. We teach the students basic programming languages like Python and JavaScript. Classes are held daily from 5-6 pm for different batches of students. In past, we have also collaborated with the Delhi Govt. and SheCodes organisation and taught more than 400 students in an offline mode. Along with that, we are also developing a self-learning website to teach animation and coding to school students using platforms like Grasshopper and Scratch. This portal consists of video tutorials and DIY activities through which the students can learn and implement their knowledge.

Viaan

VISION

The vision of this project is to ensure the holistic development of students by trying to inculcate moral education into these children as well as conducting extra-curricular activities. We also focus on leadership qualities and personality development by conducting English classes.

DESCRIPTION

Our project aims at improving the overall growth and development of underprivileged children. We try to inculcate moral education into these children as well as to conduct extra-curricular activities for them. Volunteers conduct extra-curricular activities in collaboration with other projects like VIDYA (for fun activities like diwali, christmas, independence day, etc), Munirka (for conducting best out of waste activities), munirkaRK Puram (we can conduct some activities) and also conduct english communicative classes.

UNNATI

VISION

Unnati is an initiative which aims at providing quality education of the subjects Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics to students of classes IX-X. We are currently in collaboration with Saraswati Seva Foundation, VIDYA NGO, as well as Edjustice NGO. We have already concluded a successful collaboration with the Udayan Care NGO as well, in which volunteers helped the students with their academics. We hope to continue helping our students enhance their knowledge, put them on the right path for their future and help them achieve new heights.

DESCRIPTION

Online classes were arranged for the beneficiary students of Vidya NGO, SSF and Edjustice. The classes were of 1-hour duration, 6 days a week with 2 classes per day. Volunteers took the classes from Monday to Saturday on Google Meet of the subjects Maths, Physics and Chemistry. After each class, the volunteer had to fill a form with the details of what was taught in the class. Each week’s timetable was updated and informed to the volunteers well in advance so they could contact us for any changes.

Munirka Teaching Project

VISION

The Munirka project aims for the upliftment of the slum community by making it self-reliant in various spheres with a focus on children in the community. Our main goal is to teach the kids from Munirka. Along with this we’ll also arrange some extracurricular activities that will help in the over all development of the kids.

DESCRIPTION

Regular visits (4-5 days a week) were conducted to Munirka Basti. Each visit had about 5-6 volunteers who taught the kids there and also had some co-curricular activities. A one-month long summer volunteering and 15 days of regular winter volunteering was also conducted. A special event was the cleanliness drive in Munirka with the help of kids there. NSS IITD volunteers also prepared a dance with the kids at Munirka. The kids performed the dance in IIT Delhi during Social Convention, Kaizen 2020. In this tenure, we can continue with the past activities along with adding some new and interesting activities. We’ll try to keep this activities fun and helpful for the development of those children.

Bachpan

VISION

This project aims to provide basic education opportunities to children of construction workers.

DESCRIPTION

With the help of volunteers, we make assignments and help children learn concepts like Science, Alphabets and pronunciation. The team also contacts the children of construction workers from various sites in IIT Delhi campus and the KV in the campus to collaborate with them and conduct the various activities. Along with academics, we also focus on teaching extra-curricular activities like music, dance, drawing and much more.

Food 4 Thought

VISION

The mission of the Food 4 Thought project is to improve the lives of underprivileged children and teenagers by providing them with opportunities to learn English through the medium of entertaining storybooks/course books and lots of fun activities.

DESCRIPTION

We have collaborated with NGOs where kids from classes 2nd–10th are taught by our volunteers on any subject of their choice. We are going to conduct offline classes for these kids, in which they will be taught story books and basic spoken English. We also plan some recreational activities for them. Any other suggestions or ideas are welcome from the executives! Further ideas will be discussed and implemented as per the suggestions of the executives.
