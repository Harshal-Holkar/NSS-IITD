Project Ankuram

Adult education, distinct from child education, is a practice in which adults engage in systematic and sustained self-educating activities in order to gain new forms of knowledge, skills, attitudes, or values. By implementing adult literacy programmes, this project intends to improve an individual's quality of life and enable him or her to realise his or her potential for self-realisation and elevate their level of living. The curriculum includes courses such as Speaking English, Computer and Technology, Gender Awareness, and others.

E-Novate

VISION

“Water is the elixir of life”. E-Novate aims to spread awareness & knowledge about the importance of water as a vulnerable natural resource and, thereby, the need for its conservation.

DESCRIPTION

We look for innovative and scalable ways to combat problems like water pollution. We conduct various surveys, quizzes, and interactive competitions to identify the scope and exact problem to work on and to sensitize the people about the same. Furthermore, we are planning to conduct many offline activities like distribution of aerators, mulching, tree planting, etc.

Green Warrior + E-Waste

VISION

The project Green Warriors is aimed towards the betterment of the environment by raising awareness and by conducting on-ground activities to make the campus greener. We also focus on the less-known issues like the effects of E-waste and employ innovative methods to spread the message of a cleaner and greener planet.

DESCRIPTION

In this project, we sensitize people about environment conservation sustainability issues as well as present solutions to their day-to-day wastage problems and connect them to nature. Along with a group of enthusiastic volunteers, we organise plantation drives, E-Waste collection drives, webinars, competitions and DIY activities related to the environment domain to tackle environmental issues.
